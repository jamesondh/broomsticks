  • Comments: how do you get the people to fly in your game broomsticks?

    The program redraws the field over and over very quickly. Each time the field is redrawn, the positions of the players are updated. The positions are calculated with some simple physics. If you hit the up key you get some upward velocity. If you don't touch anything then you begin to accelerate downward (gravity). Since you are flying, the players start to float down rather than drop like rocks. :-)
