Broomsticks
by Paul Rajlich

Here is a fun little applet inspired by the game Quiddich from the Harry Potter series. To play, first click on the applet. Then, control player 1 using the arrow keys. Control player 2 using the E, S, and F keys. It's easier to just click on the keys rather than hold them down. Click on your up key several times to start flying. Also, you can change your player using 1 or ENTER. If it runs choppy, try clicking on start again.

The rules of the game are:

  1. When two players collide, the player that is lower is bumped to the bottom of the screen.
  2. When a player collides with a black ball, the player is bumped.
  3. When a player gets close to the red ball, the player catches the ball.
  4. When a player puts the red ball in the opponent's basket, 10 points are scored.
