You don't have a default e-mail program set for your computer. What happens when you click the e-mail this person button is it takes the address and sends it to your default e-mail program. You can still e-mail them with out it though. Right click on the link to e-mail them, then click copy shortcut. You can go to your Yahoo or Hotmail or what and paste it (right click in the place where the address goes and click paste, or just push ctrl+v) and then you can delete the mailto: part at the beginning. Also when you hover the mouse over the link to the address you can see in the bottom left what it's going to, in this case it would say mailto:cubee2006@yahoo.com for me.
