The best way to represent this is by making three flat fields, and laying them side by side to show the three levels. BEFORE EACH PLAYER PLAYS Each Bludger moves one square closer to the nearest Seeker. The Quaffle stays still. Then, see whose side the Quaffle is in. This person plays first (if the Quaffle is in the middle, flip a coin.) Then the other player plays. Repeat the process until the Snitch is caught. ON YOUR TURN You may: 1. Move your pieces. 2. Play cards. 3. Replenish your hand to 7 cards. MOVING YOUR PIECES Each Chaser and Beater may move one space in any direction. Chasers may move two, and they may change direction in mid-move. The Keeper must stay in the Goal-Box. If the Quaffle is NOT in anyone’s possession, and is in the same space as one of your Chasers, it is taken by your Chaser and is now in your possession. Other players can’t touch the Quaffle. If the Bludger is in the same space as a Beater, it is hit two spaces in any direction. If any other player is in the same space as a Bludger, they must drop the Quaffle if they have it and get knocked one space in the same direction the Bludger hit them (of course they can’t leave the field). PLAYING A CARD Cards are really important. Without cards you can’t pass, steal, or shoot! Here are the cards: DIVE A movement boost card. Any Chaser or Beater may move straight down one extra space (Seekers two!). SPRINT A movement boost card. Any Chaser or Beater may move one extra space in any horizontal direction (no diagonals, sorry!)(Seekers two!) ROCKET A movement boost card. Any Chaser or Beater may move straight up one space (Seekers two!) PASS A passing card. You may transfer the Quaffle to any other Chaser one space in any horizontal direction and two spaces in any downward direction (diagonals are OK!)(no upward passes, sorry!) GREAT PASS A passing card. You may transfer the Quaffle to any other Chaser one space in any upwards direction, two spaces in any horizontal direction and three spaces in any downward direction (diagonals are OK!) STEAL You may take possession of the Quaffle if one of your Chasers is in the same box as an opposing Chaser carrying the Quaffle, or you may take possession of the Quaffle if one of your Chasers is in the same box as a Chaser who is receiving a pass, or you may take possession of the Quaffle if one of your Chasers is in any box through which the Quaffle is being passed. This card may be played at any time! SHOOT This card may be played at any time when one of your Chasers, carrying the Quaffle, is in the Goal-Box. Simply pick a number between one and three. Your opponent does the same. If you picked different numbers, you score ten Points and the Quaffle reappears in the center of the pitch (level 2, width 3, length 5)! If you picked the same number, the Keeper blocks the Quaffle and it becomes the possession of the first enemy Chaser to fly into that half of the field. SNITCH When you get this card, the Snitch appears. Don’t tell your opponent you have this card! The card will give a set of coordinates in this order: height, length, width. The first Seeker to enter this square earns 150 points for his team, and the game ends. There is only ONE Snitch card! IF your opponent ever stumbles into a square which borders the Snitch square, you MUST show him/her your Snitch card. If they stumble into the Snitch square itself, of course they catch the Snitch! To make the Snitch random, make ten Snitch cards before each game and hide one in the deck. Whoever has the highest score when the Snitch is caught wins!
